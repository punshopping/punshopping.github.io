1. Who we are

PunShopping ("ปันช๊อปปิ้ง", "we", "our") operates this website and an internal tool that publishes short promotional videos to our own TikTok account using the TikTok Content Posting API.

2. Data we collect

This website does not collect personal data, does not use cookies for tracking, and does not run analytics.

Our publishing tool accesses only the shop owner's own TikTok account via TikTok's official OAuth. It receives basic profile information (user.info.basic) and the ability to upload and publish videos (video.upload, video.publish) for that one account only. We do not collect or process data about any other TikTok users.

3. How data is stored

Access tokens are stored locally on the shop owner's own computer and are not shared with any third party.

4. Data sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer any data to third parties.
